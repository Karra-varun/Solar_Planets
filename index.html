🌕

A toxic, greenhouse-effect world with surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead.

About Our Neighborhood

The Solar System formed 4.6 billion years ago from the gravitational collapse of a giant interstellar molecular cloud.

Our system is located in the Orion Arm, 26,000 light-years from the center of the Milky Way galaxy. Beyond the planets lie the asteroid belt, the Kuiper belt, and the Oort cloud, home to countless icy bodies and dwarf planets.
